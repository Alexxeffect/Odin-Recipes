High Protein Mac & Cheese



This high-protein cottage cheese mac and cheese is both creamy and satisfying. It's a great option for those looking to boost their protein intake. Everythign seasoning adds a fun twist



Ingredients



  • 1/2 cup high protein elbow pasta, such as Banza elbows chickpea
  • 1/2 cup high protein cottage cheese
  • 1 ounce Cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/2 ounce Parmigiano Reggiano cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese powder (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons high protein 2% milk
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons everything seasoning blend




Directions



  1. Step 1
    Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook until tender with a bite, about 7 minutes.


  2. Step 2
    Meanwhile in a food processor or high-powered blender add cottage cheese, Cheddar cheese, Parmigiano Reggiano, Cheddar cheese powder, Worchestershire, Dijon, and milk. Season with salt and pepper. Blend until smooth


  3. Step 3
    Drain pasta, and return pasta to pan. Pour in cheese sauce. Add everything bagel seasoning; stir to combine. Serve immediately
